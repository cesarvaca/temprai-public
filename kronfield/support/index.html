Kronfield

Your calendar, in colour.

ABOUT

Private by design.

Kronfield is a native iOS calendar built around a single idea: colour is the fastest way to read your schedule. Every event belongs to a category, and every category has a colour — one glance at the month view tells you what kind of week you're having.

Your data never leaves your device. Kronfield has no cloud accounts, no subscriptions, and no external calendar sync. Everything is stored locally using SwiftData. There is no server receiving your events, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Where is my data stored? +

All of your events and categories are stored locally on your device using SwiftData, Apple's native persistence framework. Nothing is uploaded to a server, and nothing leaves your device. If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it — there is no cloud backup.

Do I need an account or subscription? +

No. Kronfield is a one-time purchase — no account required, no recurring fee, no sign-in. Buy it once and it's yours.

Can I sync my calendar across devices? +

Not currently. Kronfield is intentionally local-only — your data lives on the device you use it on. Cross-device sync is not available, and there is no iCloud or external calendar integration.

How do I add or change categories? +

Open Settings, then tap a category to edit it. You can rename it and pick a colour from a palette of 18 options. New categories can be created from the same screen. Each event is assigned to exactly one category.

How do I search my events? +

Use the search bar at the top of the main view. Search matches against event titles, category names, and notes. Results update as you type.

Is Kronfield accessible? +

Yes. Kronfield is built with full VoiceOver support — every element is labelled and navigable by screen reader. It also includes custom accessibility actions for common tasks and haptic feedback throughout the app for a tactile, eyes-free experience.

What iOS version is required? +

Kronfield requires iOS 18.0 or later. It is designed for iPhone and does not currently support iPad or macOS.
